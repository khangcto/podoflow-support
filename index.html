Getting Started

  1. Open the app and choose a duration on the Timer tab.
  2. Tap Play to start a focus session.
  3. Use Pause, Stop, or Skip as needed.
  4. Take Short and Long breaks when prompted.

FAQ

Does Podo Flow require an account?
No. Everything is local on your device.

Where is my data stored?
On your device only. Delete the app to remove it.

Why don't I see Live Activity?
Requires iOS 16.1+. Ensure notifications are allowed and the timer is running.

Troubleshooting

  • No sound: Check iPhone mute switch and volume. Make sure Focus/Do Not Disturb isn't silencing alerts.
  • Live Activity not updating: Lock the screen once after starting the timer; it should appear at the bottom.
  • Crashes or bugs: Please contact us with steps to reproduce (see Contact below).
